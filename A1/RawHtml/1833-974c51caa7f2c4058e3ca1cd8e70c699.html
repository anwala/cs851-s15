Intel® Innovations Made This

Intel® RealSense™ snapshot adds a new dimension to your tablet: depth perception. Tablets with Intel® RealSense™ snapshot have three camera lenses instead of just one. When you take a picture, the three lenses work together to create one combined photo that includes depth perception. This "depth mapping" captures the people and things in your pictures in their own space, relative to each other. That gives you the power to make the most of your photos: Change focus, switch perspectives, take measurements, and more.

You can do amazing things with Intel® RealSense™ snapshot. This is where it all changes.
