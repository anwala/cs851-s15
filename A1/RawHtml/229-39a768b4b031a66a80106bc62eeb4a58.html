One-click referencing

RefME automates the whole referencing process for you. You can search by book/journal article title, DOI, ISBN, ISSN or simply copy and paste any website URL to create a reference in seconds.

In any style

Reference in over 6,500 styles including Harvard, APA and MLA, we've got them all! We are also constantly updating and adding more styles as we find them.

Scan book and journal Barcodes

Our award winning app syncs with RefME.com and allows you to scan book or journal barcodes with PDF, OCR and much more coming soon!

Anytime, anywhere

Access your work from anywhere. Your projects, citations and bibliographies are stored in the cloud and synced across all of your devices, ready for when you are.

Works great with other tools

Import and export your work to Microsoft Word, Evernote, Mendeley and many more applications.

There's more to come!

Recommended Research, PDF annotation, OCR and much more is on the way. We think it's going to remove the search from research. Watch this space.
